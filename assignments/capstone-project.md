# Capstone Project

## Goal

Complete a full Bayesian research workflow on a meaningful applied question.

## Project Requirements

Your project must include:

1. A clear research question.
2. A dataset.
3. A likelihood appropriate for the outcome.
4. At least two priors with justification.
5. Posterior computation.
6. Diagnostics.
7. Posterior predictive check.
8. Sensitivity analysis.
9. Clear interpretation.
10. Reproducible code.

## Recommended Topics

- Transportation safety
- Travel behavior
- EV adoption
- Public transit reliability
- Logistics risk
- Education outcomes
- Survey research
- Health risk prediction
- Environmental exposure

## Final Report Structure

```text
Title
Abstract
Research question
Data
Model
Priors
Computation
Diagnostics
Posterior results
Predictive checks
Sensitivity analysis
Discussion
Limitations
References
Appendix with code
```

## Presentation Format

Prepare 8 to 10 slides:

1. Problem and motivation
2. Data
3. Model
4. Priors
5. Posterior results
6. Diagnostics
7. Predictive checks
8. Decision or implication
9. Limitations
10. Conclusion
