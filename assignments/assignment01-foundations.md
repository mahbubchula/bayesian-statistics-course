# Assignment 1: Bayesian Foundations

## Goal

Show that you understand Bayesian reasoning, conditional probability, and the relationship between prior, likelihood, and posterior.

## Tasks

1. Choose one applied research question.
2. Identify one unknown parameter.
3. Define a possible prior for that parameter.
4. Describe what data would be collected.
5. Write the likelihood in words and notation.
6. Explain what the posterior would answer.
7. Solve one diagnostic probability problem using Bayes theorem.

## Diagnostic Probability Problem

A screening tool identifies high-risk drivers. Assume:

- 5% of drivers are truly high risk.
- The tool correctly identifies 85% of high-risk drivers.
- The tool incorrectly flags 12% of low-risk drivers.

Compute:

```text
P(high risk | flagged)
```

Then explain why the result may surprise decision makers.

## Deliverable

Submit a 2 to 3 page report with equations, explanation, and one paragraph about practical meaning.
